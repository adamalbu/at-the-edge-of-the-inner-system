# At the Edge of the Inner System
A simple top-down arcade-style game about mining infinite asteroids, built in Godot 4 in GDscript.
## Playing the Game
When you first load the game, you are put in an infinite world with nothing but asteroids and the green starting area. Use W, A, S and D (or the arrow keys) to move around or turn, and use space to mine asteroids. The drill has a range, indicated by the green ring around the ship. Your goal is to made as much money as you can by mining asteroids, without crashing into them. This is made easier by purchasing upgrades in the upgrade tree, accessed by ending a run. To end a run, move back to the starting area (green circle where you spawn) and hold down `E`.

## The Upgrades
 - Thrust: Increases the ship's thrust for faster movement.
 - Stabilizers: Increases the dampening of turning, making moving slightly slower but allowing for faster stopping.
 - Torque: Like `Thrust`, but for turning
 - Gyroscope: Like `Stabilizers`, but for turning
 - Drill: Increases the range and damage of the mining drill. It is the only upgrade that can only be upgraded once.
 - Range: Increases the range of the mining drill.
 - Damage: Increases the damage of the mining drill.
